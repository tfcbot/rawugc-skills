# Yapper UGC Video Formats - Reference

| #  | Format                        | Vibe                                                              |
|----|-------------------------------|-------------------------------------------------------------------|
| 1  | The Car Rant                  | Passionate, unfiltered, and direct rant in a car.                 |
| 2  | The Mid-Meal Monologue        | Casual, relatable, and slightly messy talk while eating.          |
| 3  | The Cooking Confessional      | Intimate, messy, and genuine story while cooking.                 |
| 4  | The Distracted Diatribe       | Chaotic, funny, and very real story with constant distractions.   |
| 5  | The Walking and Talking Head  | Energetic, fast-paced, and in-the-moment talk while walking.      |
| 6  | The Bathroom Mirror Monologue | Introspective, personal, and raw talk to one's reflection.        |
| 7  | The Late-Night Thoughts       | Sleepy, intimate, and unfiltered talk in bed.                     |
| 8  | The "Just Got Out of the Shower" Rant | Fresh, energetic, and spontaneous rant after a shower.            |
| 9  | The "I'm So Over This" Complaint | Relatable, funny, and a little bit dramatic complaint.            |
| 10 | The "I Can't Believe I'm Admitting This" Confession | Secretive, funny, and vulnerable confession.                      |
| 11 | The "Hot Take" Debate         | Bold, opinionated, and engaging debate on a controversial topic.  |
| 12 | The "I'm a Genius" Hack       | Proud, excited, and helpful sharing of a clever hack.             |
| 13 | The "Unboxing Gone Wrong" Story | Humorous, self-deprecating, and relatable story of a bad unboxing.|
| 14 | The "I'm Obsessed" Rave Review | Enthusiastic, passionate, and convincing gushing over a product.  |
| 15 | The "A Day in the Life" Montage | Authentic, aspirational, and lifestyle-focused montage.           |
