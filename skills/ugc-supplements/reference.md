# Supplements UGC Formats — Shot List Reference

Shot-by-shot breakdowns for all 15 supplement UGC formats with example products filled in. For prompt templates with `[BRACKETED]` placeholders, see [SKILL.md](SKILL.md). For full completed prompts, see [examples.md](examples.md).

**Defaults**: `aspectRatio: "portrait"`, `nFrames: "10"` unless noted.

---

## 1. Morning Stack

**Vibe**: Laying out all supplements on the counter — taking them one by one with coffee.

**Example Product**: Athletic Greens AG1

- 0-3s: Kitchen counter, coffee machine dripping in the background, four supplement bottles lined up in a row — fish oil, vitamin D, magnesium, and the AG1 pouch
- 3-6s: Opening each bottle methodically — pop two fish oil capsules, one vitamin D, two magnesium — swallow with water, practiced routine
- 6-8s: AG1 gets the main moment — tearing the single-serve packet, pouring the green powder into a glass, adding water, stirring with a spoon
- 8-10s: Take a full sip of the green drink, follow with a sip of coffee — energized nod, the morning stack is complete, heading out

**Settings**: `aspectRatio: "portrait"`, `nFrames: "10"`

## 15. The Water Drop

**Vibe**: Dropping powder/liquid supplement into water — watching it dissolve, swirl, transform.

**Example Product**: LMNT Electrolytes (Citrus Salt)

- 0-3s: A clear glass of water on a white kitchen counter, LMNT Citrus Salt packet beside it — the bright packaging contrasting the clean surface
- 3-6s: Tearing the packet open and pouring the powder into the water — the yellow-orange powder hitting the surface and beginning to dissolve on contact
- 6-8s: Watching the swirl of citrus color spread through the clear water — mesmerizing diffusion, the orange plume expanding outward from the center
- 8-10s: A quick stir with a spoon, lifting the glass — the water now a uniform bright citrus color, ready to drink; the transformation from plain water to electrolyte drink is oddly beautiful

**Settings**: `aspectRatio: "portrait"`, `nFrames: "10"`
