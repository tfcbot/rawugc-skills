# DTC UGC Formats — Shot List Reference

Shot-by-shot breakdowns for all 15 DTC UGC formats with example products filled in. For prompt templates with `[BRACKETED]` placeholders, see [SKILL.md](SKILL.md). For full completed prompts, see [examples.md](examples.md).

**Defaults**: `aspectRatio: "portrait"`, `nFrames: "10"` unless noted.

---

## 1. Unboxing Moment

**Vibe**: Opening the package — tissue paper, first touch, genuine reaction to the experience.

**Example Product**: Caraway Cookware Set

- 0-3s: Caraway shipping box on a kitchen table, hands pulling at the branded tape, lifting the lid off
- 3-6s: Custom cardboard inserts revealed, lifting the cork trivets first, then unwrapping the sage green ceramic pan from its protective sleeve
- 6-8s: Picking up the pan with both hands, turning it over — feeling the weight, running a thumb across the ceramic coating, checking the handle grip
- 8-10s: Nodding slowly, impressed — the packaging engineering and the matte sage finish exceed expectations; sets it down gently, already planning dinner

**Settings**: `aspectRatio: "portrait"`, `nFrames: "10"`

## 2. My Honest Take

**Vibe**: Stream of consciousness review — product in hand, talking to camera, no script.

**Example Product**: Olive & June Nail Polish System

- 0-3s: Holding the Olive & June Mani System box on the couch, talking directly to camera — "okay so I've been using this for two weeks"
- 3-6s: Pointing at the Poppy brush handle — "this thing is genius, I can actually paint my right hand"; showing a clean manicure on their nails
- 6-8s: One honest note — "the remover pot doesn't work great on glitter, I'll be real" — shrug, not a dealbreaker
- 8-10s: Holds up the finished hand to camera — "but for at-home manis? Nothing comes close" — genuine nod, verdict delivered

**Settings**: `aspectRatio: "portrait"`, `nFrames: "10"`

## 8. Expectation vs Reality

## 9. The Collection

**Example Product**: Aesop Hand Care Line

- 3-6s: Picking up each one in sequence — "this is the everyday wash," "this is the exfoliating one for deeper clean," "this goes on after"
- 6-8s: Demonstrating the routine — pump of Resurrection, wash, dry, then a dot of the Balm rubbed into hands — the system in action
- 8-10s: Final shot of all three back on the marble shelf — amber glass, black text, white marble; cohesive, beautiful, the brand has a clear vision

## 10. Packing an Order POV

- 8-10s: Close the box, tape it with branded kraft tape, peel a shipping label and press it flat — the package is ready; every detail intentional

## 15. Side by Side Dupe Test

**Vibe**: Comparing product to a mainstream alternative — honest, fair, real.

**Example Product**: Bala Bangles vs generic ankle weights

- 0-3s: Both products on a clean counter — Bala Bangles in sage on the left, generic black Velcro ankle weights from Amazon on the right
- 3-6s: Testing the generic first — strapping it on, the Velcro catches on legging fabric, the weight distribution feels uneven, adjusting it twice
- 6-8s: Switching to Bala — the silicone-wrapped design slips on cleanly, the weight is evenly distributed, the clasp is magnetic and stays put through movement
- 8-10s: Standing side by side comparison: "these dig in, these feel like wearing a bracelet" — Bala wins on comfort and design; the comparison was fair, making the conclusion trustworthy

**Settings**: `aspectRatio: "portrait"`, `nFrames: "10"`
