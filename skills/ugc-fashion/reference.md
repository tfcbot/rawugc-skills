# Fashion UGC Formats — Shot List Reference

Shot-by-shot breakdowns for all 20 fashion UGC formats with example brands filled in. For prompt templates with `[BRACKETED]` placeholders, see [SKILL.md](SKILL.md). For full completed prompts, see [examples.md](examples.md).

**Defaults**: `aspectRatio: "portrait"`, `nFrames: "10"` unless noted.

---

## 1. Mirror OOTD

**Vibe**: Classic mirror outfit check — simple spin, natural lighting, main character energy.

**Example Brand**: Everlane

- 0-3s: Standing in front of a full-length bedroom mirror, phone visible in the reflection, wearing an Everlane oversized blazer, wide-leg chinos, and white leather sneakers
- 3-6s: Camera slowly reveals from the white sneakers up — cuffed chinos, relaxed blazer drape, clean neckline
- 6-8s: Casual spin to show the back — blazer falls nicely, back vent visible, chinos silhouette clean from behind
- 8-10s: Faces mirror again, one hand in blazer pocket, slight confident head tilt — the look is complete

**Settings**: `aspectRatio: "portrait"`, `nFrames: "10"`

## 2. Try-On in Real Time

**Vibe**: Getting dressed on camera — genuine first reaction to how it fits.

- 6-8s: First look in the mirror — genuine surprise, the silhouette hugs the waist and flares slightly at the ankle

## 10. Size Honest

**Vibe**: "I'm a size X and here's how this actually fits" — genuine, no filter.

**Example Brand**: Girlfriend Collective

- 0-3s: Talking to camera — "I'm a size L, 5'7", and here's how the Girlfriend Collective Compressive legging actually fits"
- 3-6s: Turning to show front view, then side, then back — the high-rise waistband, the compression through the thigh, the ankle length
- 6-8s: Pointing at specifics — "the waist doesn't roll down when I bend," "the seams are flatlock so no chafing," "the length hits right at the ankle bone"
- 8-10s: Final honest verdict — thumbs up, "size up if you're between sizes, but the compression is legit"
