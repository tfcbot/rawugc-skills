# Fashion UGC Formats — Shot List Reference

Shot-by-shot breakdowns for all 20 fashion UGC formats with example brands filled in. For prompt templates with `[BRACKETED]` placeholders, see [SKILL.md](SKILL.md). For full completed prompts, see [examples.md](examples.md).

**Defaults**: `aspectRatio: "portrait"`, `nFrames: "10"` unless noted.

---

## 1. Mirror OOTD

**Vibe**: Classic mirror outfit check — simple spin, natural lighting, main character energy.

**Example Brand**: Everlane

- 0-3s: Standing in front of a full-length bedroom mirror, phone visible in the reflection, wearing an Everlane oversized blazer, wide-leg chinos, and white leather sneakers
- 3-6s: Camera slowly reveals from the white sneakers up — cuffed chinos, relaxed blazer drape, clean neckline
- 6-8s: Casual spin to show the back — blazer falls nicely, back vent visible, chinos silhouette clean from behind
- 8-10s: Faces mirror again, one hand in blazer pocket, slight confident head tilt — the look is complete

**Settings**: `aspectRatio: "portrait"`, `nFrames: "10"`

---

## 2. Try-On in Real Time

**Vibe**: Getting dressed on camera — genuine first reaction to how it fits.

**Example Brand**: Aritzia

- 0-3s: Holding up Aritzia Melina pants on a wooden hanger — the vegan leather catching the light, showing them flat to camera
- 3-6s: Stepping into the high-waisted pants, pulling them up, zipping the side zipper
- 6-8s: First look in the mirror — genuine surprise, the silhouette hugs the waist and flares slightly at the ankle
- 8-10s: Smoothing the front, turning to check the side profile, satisfied nod — they fit even better than expected

**Settings**: `aspectRatio: "portrait"`, `nFrames: "10"`

## 3. 3 Ways to Style

**Vibe**: Same piece, 3 completely different outfits — quick transitions.

**Example Brand**: COS (white oversized button-down)

- 0-2s: Look 1 — COS button-down tucked into high-waisted jeans with white Converse, sleeves rolled, casual weekend
- 2-5s: Quick snap transition
- 5-7s: Look 2 — same shirt half-tucked into a black midi skirt with heeled mules and gold hoops, going-out energy
- 7-10s: Quick snap transition
- 10-12s: Look 3 — shirt unbuttoned and layered open over a black slip dress with combat boots, unexpected edge
- 12-15s: Rapid flash: Look 1, Look 2, Look 3 — one shirt, three lives

**Settings**: `aspectRatio: "portrait"`, `nFrames: "15"`

## 5. Fit Check Walk

**Vibe**: Walking toward camera in the outfit — street or hallway, confident energy.

## 7. Day to Night Transition

## 10. Size Honest

**Vibe**: "I'm a size X and here's how this actually fits" — genuine, no filter.

**Example Brand**: Girlfriend Collective

- 0-3s: Talking to camera — "I'm a size L, 5'7", and here's how the Girlfriend Collective Compressive legging actually fits"
- 3-6s: Turning to show front view, then side, then back — the high-rise waistband, the compression through the thigh, the ankle length
- 6-8s: Pointing at specifics — "the waist doesn't roll down when I bend," "the seams are flatlock so no chafing," "the length hits right at the ankle bone"
- 8-10s: Final honest verdict — thumbs up, "size up if you're between sizes, but the compression is legit"

**Settings**: `aspectRatio: "portrait"`, `nFrames: "10"`

## 20. Returns vs Keeps

**Vibe**: Trying on multiple items, sorting honestly into keep and return piles.

**Example Brand**: Abercrombie (keeper piece)

- 0-2s: Five items from different brands spread on the bed — tags still on, tissue paper nearby
- 2-5s: First item: a fast-fashion dress — try on, head shake, "the fabric feels cheap"; toss to the return pile
- 5-8s: Second item: an online impulse buy jacket — "weird fit in the shoulders"; return pile. Third: try on the Abercrombie Sloane tailored pant — face lights up, the drape is perfect, the waist sits exactly right
- 8-11s: Two more items tried — one keep, one return. The sorting is honest and quick.
- 11-15s: Final shot: the keep pile — three items, the Abercrombie Sloane pant front and center; the returns stacked in the shipping bag; product earned its spot

**Settings**: `aspectRatio: "portrait"`, `nFrames: "15"`
